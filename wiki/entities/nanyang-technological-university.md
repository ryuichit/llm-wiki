# Nanyang Technological University

A leading research university in Singapore, contributing to cutting-edge research in LLM security and web agent defense systems.

## Overview

Nanyang Technological University (NTU) is one of the primary institutions behind the [[webcloak-paper|WebCloak research]], focused on addressing emerging security threats from AI-driven web agents.

## Associated Researchers

- [[xinfeng-li|Xinfeng Li]] - First author of WebCloak paper
- [[wei-dong|Wei Dong]] - Corresponding author

## Research Contributions

### WebCloak Project

NTU researchers led the development of [[webcloak|WebCloak]], a groundbreaking defense system against [[llm-driven-web-agents|LLM-driven web scrapers]]. The research:

- Characterized three paradigms of LLM scraping: [[llm-to-script|L2S]], [[llm-native-crawlers|LNC]], and [[llm-based-web-agents|LWA]]
- Created [[llmcrawlbench|LLMCrawlBench]] with 237 webpages and 10,895 images
- Demonstrated complete protection (0% scraping recall) while maintaining user experience
- Established the [[agent-as-attacker|agent-as-attacker paradigm]] in security research

## Research Areas

- Adversarial machine learning
- Intelligent agent systems
