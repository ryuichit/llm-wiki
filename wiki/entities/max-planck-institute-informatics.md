## Key Publications

**[[robots-txt-gatekeeping-paper|Is Misinformation More Open? A Study of robots.txt Gatekeeping on the Web]]** (WWW '26)
- Led by [[ha-dao|Ha Dao]] (corresponding author)
- International collaboration
- First comprehensive longitudinal study of AI crawler gatekeeping
- Analyzed 70,410 robots.txt files across 6 snapshots (Sep 2023 - May 2025)
- Public dataset: https://doi.org/10.17617/3.WW04D8

**Key Findings**:
- 60% of reputable sites block AI crawlers vs. 9.1% of misinformation sites
- Gap widening from 23% to 60% for reputable sites (Sep 2023 - May 2025)
- Implications for LLM training data quality and bias
- Demonstrates limitations of voluntary compliance protocols
