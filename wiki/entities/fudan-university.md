# Fudan University

## Overview
Leading Chinese research university based in Shanghai, with significant contributions to AI research, particularly in web automation, natural language processing, and LLM applications.

## Category
Organization / University

## Location
Shanghai, China

## Relevant Departments

### Shanghai Key Laboratory of Data Science
- Focus on data science research
- School of Computer Science
- Home to AUTOCRAWLER research team

### School of Data Science
- Dedicated data science education and research
- Collaborative research with computer science

### Fudan-Aishu Cognitive Intelligence Joint Research Center
- Joint research center for cognitive intelligence
- Partnership with industry (Aishu)
- Focus on applied AI research

## Notable Research

### AUTOCRAWLER Project (2024)
Web automation framework combining LLMs with crawler generation
- Lead institution for research
- Collaboration with Alibaba
- Published in arXiv (2404.12753v1)
