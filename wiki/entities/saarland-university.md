# Saarland University

Major research university in Saarbrücken, Germany, with strong focus on computer science, web systems, and data ethics.

## Overview

**Location**: Saarbrücken, Germany

**Type**: Public research university

**Focus Areas**: Computer science, informatics, web systems, security, data ethics
