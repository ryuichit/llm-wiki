# University of Hawaii at Manoa

A U.S. research university contributing to cutting-edge security research on LLM-driven web agents and content protection systems.

## Overview

The University of Hawaii at Manoa is one of the collaborating institutions on the [[webcloak-paper|WebCloak research]], focusing on mitigating threats from AI-driven web scraping.

## Research Contributions

### WebCloak Project

Collaborated on the systematic study of [[llm-driven-web-agents|LLM-driven scrapers]], contributing to:

- Characterization of three LLM scraping paradigms
- Evaluation methodology for [[llmcrawlbench|LLMCrawlBench]]
- Development of [[semantic-labyrinth|semantic labyrinth]] defense techniques
- Analysis of scraping democratization effects

## Collaborations

The WebCloak research was conducted jointly with:
- [[nanyang-technological-university|Nanyang Technological University]] (lead institution)
- [[hong-kong-polytechnic-university|Hong Kong Polytechnic University]]
