# University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign

Major research university in Illinois, home to research on web security, LLM-powered automation, and scraping democratization.

## Location

Urbana-Champaign, Illinois, USA

## Research Contributions

### Beyond BeautifulSoup Project

Lead institution for the [[beyond-beautifulsoup-paper|Beyond BeautifulSoup research]], with affiliated researchers:
- [[gang-wang|Gang Wang]] (Professor)
- [[nirav-diwan|Nirav Diwan]] (Researcher)

The research evaluated the democratization of web scraping through LLM-powered tools, measuring what non-expert users can accomplish across 35 websites spanning five security tiers.

## Research Areas

- Web security and privacy
- Anti-bot mechanisms
- LLM-powered automation
- Threat modeling and defense
- CAPTCHA and authentication systems
- Automated browsing detection
