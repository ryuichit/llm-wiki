# Hong Kong Polytechnic University

A leading research institution in Hong Kong contributing to security research on LLM-driven web agents and defense systems.

## Overview

Hong Kong Polytechnic University is one of the collaborating institutions on the [[webcloak-paper|WebCloak research]], working to understand and mitigate threats from [[llm-driven-web-agents|LLM-driven web scrapers]].

## Research Contributions

### WebCloak Project

Collaborated on the comprehensive study of LLM scraping threats, contributing to:

- Evaluation of 32 scraper implementations across three paradigms
- Development of [[llmcrawlbench|LLMCrawlBench]] benchmark
- Design and testing of [[webcloak|WebCloak]] defense mechanisms
- Analysis of the [[parse-then-interpret|parse-then-interpret vulnerability]]

## Collaborations

The WebCloak research was conducted jointly with:
- [[nanyang-technological-university|Nanyang Technological University]] (lead institution)
- [[university-of-hawaii|University of Hawaii at Manoa]]
