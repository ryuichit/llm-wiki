# University of Notre Dame

Lead research institution for the [[autodata-paper|AutoData project]], contributing to multi-agent systems and automated web data collection research.

## Location

Notre Dame, Indiana, United States

## Research Contributions

### AutoData Project

University of Notre Dame served as the lead institution for developing:
- [[autodata|AutoData]] multi-agent system
- [[ohcache|OHCache]] oriented hypergraph cache
- [[instruct2ds|Instruct2DS]] benchmark
- Novel coordination mechanisms for multi-agent web data collection
