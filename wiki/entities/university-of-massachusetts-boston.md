Public research university located in Boston, USA. Lead institution for [[web-agent-rl-constraints-paper|constrained agentic reinforcement learning research]].

Host institution for [[qianli-ma|Qianli Ma]] (corresponding author) on breakthrough constrained RL work for web agents, addressing real-world deployment challenges including:
